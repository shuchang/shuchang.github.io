Biography

Chang Shu is a MS student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST). He is a member of the KAUST Robotics, Intelligent Systems, and Control Lab (KAUST RISC Lab), supervised by Dr. Shinkyu Park. Currently, he is working on solving the combinatorial optimzation problem of robot manipulation tasks with graph-based reinforcement learning.

Prior to KAUST, he obtained a joint BEng (First Class Honours) at University of Glasgow and also a joint BEng at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
